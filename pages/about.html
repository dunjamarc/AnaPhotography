Welcome to the virtual art gallery of photographer Ana Trkulja.

Ana is Zemun based artist, whose passion is taking pictures, capturing moments, and making memories through photographs. In this gallery you can enjoy her work made by her four main inspirations: her home city Zemun, magnificent skylines, architecture frames taken on her travel, and various photos edited in black & white technique. There is much more to see on her Instagram and Facebook accounts.
